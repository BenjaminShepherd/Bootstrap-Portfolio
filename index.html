About Me

My name is Ben Shepherd. I was born and raised in Salt Lake City, Utah and grew up loving the Wasatch Mountains and sports. I went to the University of Utah, and received my Bachelors Degree in Interpersonal Communication. I love going to Lake Powell with family and friends, my favorite sports teams are the Atlanta Braves, Utah Jazz, & the Utah Utes. I like all kinds of music, most kinds of food, and have a goofy and funny personality.
